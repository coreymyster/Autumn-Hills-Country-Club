Font

My application utilizes the "Hindi" and "sans-serif" font-families with charcoal text in the body as well as colored text utilizing colors from the "color theme" in the headers. Additionally, some of the headers will use a serif font named "Cormorant Garamond" to achieve the classy effect of a country club setting.
